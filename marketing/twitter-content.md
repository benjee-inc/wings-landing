## Content Rules
- Always include a link to the waitlist in CTA posts
- Keep posts under 280 characters when possible (unless it's a thread)
- Use line breaks for readability
- No hashtag spam — 2-3 max, and only relevant ones
- Respond to every reply within 24 hours
- Retweet/engage with others building in the AI/productivity space
